With this app you can receive notifications from your PreCom app.
It adds one trigger card for flows and has the ability to transfer the message to your own flows.

How to install and use:
- Go to your Homey App
- Open Settings
- Open PreCom Settings
- Copy the Webhook URL displayed in the app
- Paste this into the PreCom App
- And wait for an emergency call. (and don't stay at home!)
